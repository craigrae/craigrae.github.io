About us

Cakes & Bakes opened its oven door to the public in 2014 and have since been providing delicious treats to our lovely customers from out home bakery in the west end of Glasgow. We make bespoke cakes and bakes to order and also sell our wares at local markets and events in Glasgow. We cater for all occasions: events, weddings, birthdays, Christmas, christenings or even providing a dessert to impress guests at a dinner party, whatever your whim, we can bake it. Specialising in bespoke cake design we aim to work with you to create a personal, individual, special and, most importantly, delicious cake that is perfect for you. We don't scrimp on ingredients, using the best we can get our hands on including free range eggs, finest vanilla bean paste, organic flour and quality fair trade chocolate, to name a few. At Cakes & Bakes every bake is a showstopper challenge!

Layer cakes

Layer cakes can be created in any flavour combination you can dream of. Popular sponges include: vanilla, chocolate, red velvet, lemon, coffee, sticky toffee and carrot cake. Mix these with your choice of buttercream (vanilla, chocolate, caramel, peppermint, cream cheese...) throw in some jam if you wish then finish off with delicate handmade sugarpaste flowers, figures or even some 24 carat edible gold and you have your dream cake! Cakes can be made in any design, any flavour and any size.

Cupcakes

Similar to our layer1cakes, cupcakes can be made in any flavour with any design. A minimum order of 12 cupcakes is required for each flavour, however these can be decorated differently if desired.
